PASS <key> : Sets a connection password. This command must be sent before the NICK/USER registration combination.
NICK <nickname> : Allows a client to change their IRC nickname.
JOIN <channels> [<keys>] : Makes the client join the channels in the comma-separated list <channels>, specifying the passwords, if needed, in the comma-separated list <keys>. If the channel(s) do not exist then they will be created.
